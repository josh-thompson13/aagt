Important Terms & Conditions

Loan Eligibility

Loans are subject to credit assessment and approval. Minimum loan amount $150,000. Maximum loan amount $5,000,000. Security required for all loans.

Interest Rates

Interest rates are calculated monthly and may vary based on loan amount, term, security, and individual circumstances. Rates shown are indicative only.

Repayment Terms

Flexible repayment options available including interest-only payments during the term. Principal and interest payments can be arranged to suit cash flow requirements.

Security Requirements

All loans are secured by real estate or business assets. Professional valuations are required for all security properties.

Responsible Lending

We are committed to responsible lending practices. All applications are assessed for suitability and affordability in accordance with regulatory requirements.
